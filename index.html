About the Shop

Tacoma's Independent Auto Specialist

Black Line Motors is an independent auto repair shop located in the heart of Tacoma, WA. We were built on a simple idea: great mechanical work, done honestly, at a fair price.

We specialize in Audi and BMW — German engineering is in our DNA — but our team is equally at home servicing any make or model on the road today.

Equipped with two professional lifts and commercial parts accounts with the industry's top suppliers, we deliver dealer-quality results without the dealer markup or the wait.
